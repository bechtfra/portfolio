The Brief

A design challenge: Redesign TripUp, a group travel and shared expense app. The deliverable was a wireflow of up to 15 screens and three high-fidelity screens.

The existing app had the core mechanics but the flows were complicated and the UI didn't make the shared-expense side feel manageable. The brief gave me room to interpret.

What I Added

Beyond the brief, I included two features: a QR-based member invite flow to simplify getting a group into the app, and automated debt minimisation, which is an algorithm that reduces the number of transactions needed to settle a group's expenses, so nobody ends up in a chain of back-and-forth payments.
