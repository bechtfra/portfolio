The Work

As lead UI designer I was responsible for the website: product launches, campaign pages, and e-commerce across three years and 40+ pages. The brief was always to make the digital experience match the quality of the hardware.

Each product launch had its own visual direction, yet the overarching goal was always to maintain a cohesive design language.

HD60 X

Strong visuals and scroll animations were used in a darker theme to showcase a product that targets a more professional creator group.

Green Screen XL

A fun and simple page that makes exploring the features of a big green product feel exciting.

Wave DX

Bold parallax galleries and full width asset animations promote the premium aspect of the product while tables and flippable cards showcase product features in detail.
