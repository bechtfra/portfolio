Playboy Deutschland  ·  2020

Website
& Webshop
Redesign

Playboy is not only a unique print brand, but also stands with its offers for an extraordinary attitude towards life. To identify itself with this statement, Playboy Germany got a complete website makeover in 2020.

Role
UI Designer
Type
Website & E-Commerce Redesign
Period
2020
The Goal

The brand had a strong visual identity in print but the digital experience hadn't kept pace. Editorial pages and the webshop had been developed separately over time and had drifted apart. They were visually inconsistent, with different layout grids, type choices, and interaction patterns.

Users moving between article content and the shop had a noticeably different experience in each context, which made the overall site feel less premium than the brand warranted.

The Approach

Starting with a shared design language, I created a common type scale, colour system, and component set that could underpin both sides of the site. The editorial templates were rebuilt to feel more intentional and magazine-like. The webshop followed the same visual rules while being adapted for product-first layouts.
