REWE Group  ·  2023–Present

toom
Baumarkt

With more than 300 stores in its portfolio, toom is one of the leading providers in the German DIY sector. The company is part of the REWE Group which is one of the leading retail and tourism groups in Germany and Europe. When our collaboration started, toom Baumarkt was ready to revamp its mobile shopping app, enhance its digital presence, and streamline their shopping experience.

Role
Design Lead
Type
Native App (iOS & Android), Design System
Period
Sept 2023–Present
The Situation

The objective was to transform a sales channel into a user friendly platform, supporting toom Baumarkt’s goals of boosting sales and expanding its user base. When I took over this project from another colleague in September 2023, maintenance and feature ideation was in progress. The goal remained to further improve the existing shopping experience and develop an ambitious roadmap for 2024 that will introduce further features to the App.

The Work

I prioritised: stabilise what was there first, then introduce new features in order of user impact. Working directly with the iOS/Android team meant faster iteration and fewer handoff gaps.

Building out a component-based design system as well as a token architecture for the apps font and typography styles gave the team a shared foundation.
